Viva Frei Wikipedia: Everything On His Career

David Freiheit was born in Montreal, Quebec, and grew up as the youngest of five siblings.

David pursued his Bachelor of Arts from McGill University after studying Creative Arts at Dawson College.

Freiheit went on to study at Université Laval, where he earned his civil law degree and served as the editor-in-chief of the university’s law journal.

Following in the footsteps of his father and older siblings, he began practicing law in 2007. David worked for the prestigious commercial litigation company Borden Ladner Gervais for six years.

He left his job to become a freelance litigator after the birth of his first child to achieve a better work-life balance. He has founded his firm, “Freiheit Legal.”

He was a practicing business attorney for the first few years when he began his YouTube career. He struggled to find time to make his videos and left his law career in January 2018 to devote his full attention to YouTube.

On July 22, 2021, David announced his intention to run for Parliament as a member of the People’s Party of Canada.
