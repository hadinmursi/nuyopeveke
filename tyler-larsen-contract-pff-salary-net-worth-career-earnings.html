Tyler Larsen Contract, PFF, Salary, Net Worth, Career Earnings

Tyler Larsen, an American football center with the Washington Football Team of the National Football League, was born on July 8, 1991. (NFL). He was a member of the Utah State football team in college. In 2014, he signed as an undrafted free agent with the Miami Dolphins, and he has also played with the Carolina Panthers.

Larsen went to Jordan High School in Sandy, Utah, where he was a lineman on both the offensive and defensive lines. As a senior, he earned first-team all-state and second-team all-region honors after posting 27 tackles, six sacks, and six fumble recoveries, four of which went for scores. All four seasons, he helped lead the Beetdiggers to the state playoffs, finishing with 96 tackles, 16 sacks, 12 fumble recoveries, and four touchdowns.

Tyler Larsen Contract

Tyler Larsen agreed to a one-year, $1,040,000 contract with the Washington Football Team, which included a $50,000 signing bonus, $50,000 in guaranteed money, and a $1,040,000 average yearly salary.
