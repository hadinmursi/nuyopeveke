Farrah Abraham Attacks Blac Chyna on Instagram, Blac Chyna Fans Fight Back

Drama, drama, drama. Farrah Abraham wrote a derogatory comment on Blac Chyna’s Instagram account on Friday, April 29, sparking what looks to be the latest in a long line of her celebrity feuds.

The Teen Mom star, 24, stirred up trouble when she not-so-discreetly commented on a short video clip of Chyna, 27, bopping along to Beyoncé’s “Sorry.”

“F–kin monkey ewe s–t come up 4 what she is a nothing,” Abraham wrote, seemingly unprompted.

Chyna’s fans immediately went into attack mode, barraging both Chyna and Abraham’s accounts with comments.

“Why are you so evil??!!???” one commenter wrote shortly after Abraham’s comment. “You are a porn star!! U came from nothing. Thank MTV and god everyday for what you have!”

Other Instagram users called the reality star “racist,” “irresponsible” and a whole slew of other names, posting monkey emojis in the comments of her photos.

Chyna has yet to publicly respond to Abraham’s slam.

In January, Abraham got into a spat with Nicki Minaj after the “Anaconda” singer made a swipe at her behavior on an episode of Teen Mom.

“Farrah is a c–t to her mother,” Minaj, 33, wrote.
