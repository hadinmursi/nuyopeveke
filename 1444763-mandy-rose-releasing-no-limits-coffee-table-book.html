Mandy Rose Releasing No Limits Coffee Table Book

The former Mandy Rose is giving fans some new reading material.

Now going by Mandy Sacs, the former WWE Superstar is set to release a new coffee table book later this month.

Mandy teamed up with FanGear for a new book called No Limits. The photo book measures 8.5 x 9 inches and it features “over 120 pages of hand picked photos and personal insights on Mandy’s life and career. This is a must-have item for all Mandy Sacs fans.”

According to the listing, the book features more than 225 images. It is printed “on premium cover stock and is shipped in a discreet package.” It also arrives at your home shrink-wrapped to “ensure a perfect delivery.”

The book will begin shipping on February 19; you can pre-order the book here.

“Thank you everyone for all the love and continued support. You are all so amazing! I am so grateful for each and every one of you! It’s because of you that I am able to live my dream and continue to bless you with all the best content,” Mandy wrote on Instagram.

A return to WWE?

During a recent episode of her Power Alphas Podcast with Sabby Piscitelli, Mandy Rose answered questions from fans. She noted that most of them asked whether she would ever want to return to WWE.

“A lot of wrestling questions, obviously. The most common, when I’m coming back to WWE, so I’ll just give a general answer,” Mandy Rose said. “I don’t know if I’ll ever be back in WWE, I don’t know what the future holds. However, I’m enjoying my life a lot right now, and we’re getting married soon.

“We got a lot of big plans, a lot of trips, a lot of exciting things in the works. So yeah, that’s your answer. You never shut doors. I will say, if I was to ever come back, it would be for my fans, for you guys. Because I know you guys miss me, and I miss you guys too.”
