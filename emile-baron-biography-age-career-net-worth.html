Emile Baron Biography, Age, Career &amp; Net Worth

Emile Baron (born 17 June 1979 in Fish Hoek, Western Cape) is a South African retired footballer who played as a goalkeeper for South Africa.

Emile Baron Age

He was born on the 17th of June 1979 in Fish Hoek, Western Cape, South Africa. As of 2021, he is 42 years old and celebrates his birthday on June 17th of every year.

International Career

Baron made his international debut in a friendly against Saudi Arabia on 20 March 2002 and has so far been capped 4 times.

He was also a participant at the 2004 African Nations Cup. In April 2010, he got a shoulder injury which caused him to miss the 2010 FIFA World Cup
