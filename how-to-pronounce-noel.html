How to pronounce noel

How do you pronounce the boys name Noel?

Noel is exclusively a boy name and is pronounced Nowl, rhymes with bowl, roll, poll, troll and toll. Noelle is a girl name and is pronounced no-elle, like the French word for Christmas.

Is Noel a Spanish name?

Noel – Meaning of Noel

Noel is used chiefly in the Spanish, English, and German languages, and it is derived from Hebrew and Latin origins. From Latin roots, its meaning is born on Christmas – in this context, Noel can be used in the English and German languages.
