    Mike Dooley Career

    Dooley studied accounting at the University of Florida and in 1983, he graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Accounting. His academic qualification led him to become a tax specialist and began his career working in Saudi Arabia, Florida, and Massachusetts for Price Waterhouse Coopers. In 1989, he collaborated with his mother, Sheelagh Mawe, who is also an author and his brother Andy Dooley, to co-found TUT Enterprises, Inc., a professional training & coaching company.

    In the beginning, TUT Enterprises (Totally Unique Thoughts) was involved in selling gifts from small chains of stores and its own line of T-shirts through a distributor located in Jyoetsu, Japan. The family company changed from its retail and wholesale transactions and transformed into an “Adventurer’s Club” that celebrates the adventure of life. Dooley was the sole shareholder of the company. After the transformation, Dooley recorded his first audio program titled, Infinite Possibilities: The Art of Living Your Dreams. It was published in 2001 and since then, it has been a perennial best-seller. Its book edition was released in 2009 and became #7 on the NY Times bestseller list.

    Mike Dooley TUT.com

    This is the website to Dooley’s philosophical Adventurers Club and it has thousands of members from over 189 countries in the world. The organization’s members receive Dooley’s daily e-mailings, “Notes from the Universe”, which have spun off into books, calendars, greeting cards, and world tours.

    Mike’s writings have been published in 25 languages and he loves traveling all the world to speak about life, happiness, and dreams.

    Today, Dooley is one of the teachers on The Secret, an international phenomenon that was started by author Rhonda Byrne. He is also the writer of the daily emails “Notes from the Universe” that are sent out five days each week. Furthermore, “Notes from the Universe” reaches more than 800,000 people.
