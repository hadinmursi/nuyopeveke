Bobby Roode is Now Known as Robert Roode

Originally published on ProWrestlingSheet.

Another WWE name change has occurred ... Bobby Roode is now known as Robert Roode. 

Roode made the announcement in a backstage interview and the name change is now reflected on his WWE.com profile. 

The 41-year-old wrestler is also sporting a fresh new mustache, ala Rick Rude. 

As we previously reported, War Raiders made their debut on Raw last week using the name The Viking Experience. However, their name was changed to The Viking Raiders earlier today on WWE.com
