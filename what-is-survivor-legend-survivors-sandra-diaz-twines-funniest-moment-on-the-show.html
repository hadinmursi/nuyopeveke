What is Survivor legend Sandra Diaz-Twine's funniest moment on the show?

When most fans think about Survivor's Sandra Diaz-Twine, they initially reflect on the amazing fact that she won two seasons of the show. After that, the next thing that almost certainly comes to mind is how hilarious Diaz-Twine can be. The perfect example of that is Diaz-Twine’s funniest moment which involved her rival Jon “Jonny Fairplay” Dalton. When Jonny Fairplay had his loved one visit, he and his pal infamously faked that his grandmother died. Instead of fawning over Jonny Fairplay, Diaz-Twine hilariously remarked that she wished she could vote his friend off the island at one point.
