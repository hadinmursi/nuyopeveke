Why did the Fugees disband?

The Fugees music group made up of Pras Michel, Wyclef Jean, and Lauryn Hill was formed in 1988 but broke up in 1997.

Some sources claim that they split up because they wanted to focus on their solo careers. However, Wycleaf disclosed that the group broke up because he had a romantic relationship with Lauryn Hill.

“I was married and Lauryn and I were having an affair, but she led me to believe that the baby was mine, and I couldn’t forgive that,” he said.

He reportedly added that the betrayal was the main reason why the band broke up.

“It was like we were two outlaws in love. We had fights on planes. We had huge fights, and a few times when it went down, she started swinging at me right there in the seats. People would scatter. We never got arrested, we came close a few times in Europe,” he added.
