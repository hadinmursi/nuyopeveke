Scott Grimes Height: How tall is Scott Grimes?

Scott Christopher Grimes, an American actor, and musician, was born on July 9, 1971, and he is well-known in both of these spheres. Throughout his career, he has taken on a range of tasks, demonstrating his talent and versatility in each.

The cult classic “The Night They Saved Christmas,” in which Grimes had a significant role, helped to launch his career in the entertainment business. Additionally, he gained recognition for his roles as Will McCorkle in “Party of Five” and Dr. Archie Morris in the popular TV program “ER.”

Grimes’ superb performance as Technical Sergeant Donald Malarkey in the acclaimed miniseries “Band of Brothers” enhanced his image as a gifted actor. He also supplied the Steve Smith voice in the animated comedy “American Dad!” As a result, he has an avid fan base.

Grimes is particularly adored by fans of cult movies for his portrayal as Bradley Brown in the first two “Critters” movies. He has been a regular cast member in the sci-fi comedy-drama “The Orville,” playing Gordon Malloy, since 2017.
