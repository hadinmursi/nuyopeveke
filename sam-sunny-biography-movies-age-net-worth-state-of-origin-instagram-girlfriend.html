Early Life

Sam Sunny hails from Ukpor Nnewi South LGA of Anambra state in the southeast region of Nigeria.

Sam Sunny was born on the 12th of April into a family of eight (8) and was raised by his parent. He is highly appreciated in the Nollywood industry for his intriguing roles in movies. He is currently in his late 20s.

Education

After completing both elementary and secondary school education, Sam Sunny furthered to Nnamdi Azikiwe University to bag a degree in Linguistics.

Career

Sam Sunny is not only a handsome person but also endowed with talents to reckon with in the entertainment industry. He has a perfect sense of interpretation of his roles.

Initially, he was not bent on becoming an actor but somehow he raw himself falling in love with the Art.

Sam Sunny has featured in several movies and they include Signs of Love, Bride of Death, Yankee Students to mention but a few.
