Who is Nicholas Turturro? Nicholas Turturro Biography and Wiki

Nicholas Turturro is an American actor, writer, and producer who is best known for his roles in New York City-based films and on the television series Blue Bloods and NYPD Blue. He is the younger brother of John Turturro and the cousin of Aida Turturro.

Nicholas Turturro Family, Parents

Turturro was born in Brooklyn, New York to Katherine Florence (Incerella), who was an amateur jazz singer, who worked in a Navy yard during World War II, and was born in the U.S., to Italian parents, with roots in Sicily. His father, Nicholas Turturro, Sr., was a carpenter and shoemaker who emigrated from Giovinazzo (Puglia), Italy.

He has two brothers who resemble just like one is artist Ralph Turturro and actor, writer, and filmmaker John Turturro. He has a cousin namely Aida Turturro and she is an actress. His two nephews are Actor Amedeo Turturro and Actor Diego Turturro. In general, all of his family members is composed of actors and actress.

Nicholas Turturro Wife

Turturro is married to Lissa Espinosa who is a Flight attendant. The couple tied the knot in 1996. Turturro’s first marriage was with Jami Biunno whom he married in 1984 until their divorce in 1995. He is blessed with two daughters Erica Turturro and Apollonia Turturro.
