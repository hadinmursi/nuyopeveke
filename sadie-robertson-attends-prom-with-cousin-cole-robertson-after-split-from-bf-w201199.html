Sadie Robertson Attends Prom With Cousin Cole Robertson After Split From BF

You can always count on family! Duck Dynasty star Sadie Robertson split from her longtime boyfriend Blake Coward a few weeks ago, as Us Weekly exclusively reported on March 30, but that didn’t stop the teen from enjoying her prom.

The 18-year-old reality star, whose parents are Willie and Korie Robertson, revealed on Saturday, April 2, that she hit up the school dance in Monroe, Louisiana, with her cousin Cole Robertson, the youngest son of Jase and Missy Robertson.

“Making mems with the fam is the best, because we can laugh about them forever. thank you for always being there cuz! couldn’t do life without you!” the Dancing With the Stars season 19 runner-up captioned a cute photo on Instagram of the pair in their black-and-white formal wear.

Sadie, who also shared a mirror selfie of her glam look, wore a gown by Sherri Hill, for whom she has done some modeling.
