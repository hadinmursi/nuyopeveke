Mila Kunis Jim Beam Spokesperson: See Her New Brand Commercial

Bourbon never looked so good. Announced as its new global partner and spokesperson last month, Mila Kunis now stars in three newly released commercials on behalf of the iconic whiskey brand Jim Beam.

“The Beam family has a long history of doing things their own way,” Kunis, 30, says in one specific ad. “They age every drop of Jim Beam twice as long as the law requires for a true Kentucky Straight Bourbon. So, four long years from now, I’ll be back for this one.”

In the clip, the Jupiter Ascending actress dresses for comfort in a jumpsuit while working in Jim Beam’s beer brewery. She has her brunette locks in a braid and swept to one side and brands her name into a barrel.

“That’s how Jim Beam makes history,” she continues in a voiceover. “How will you make yours?” (Ashton Kutcher‘s gal also stars in two other ads; one in a Kentucky backdrop “200 years” ago and another where she tries to sneak out with a Jim Beam Honey Bourbon without waking up a sleeping bear.)
