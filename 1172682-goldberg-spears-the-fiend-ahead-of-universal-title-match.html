Goldberg Spears The Fiend Ahead Of Universal Title Match

Goldberg finally made his way to the Friday Night SmackDown ring, and he and The Fiend even had a meeting in the middle of the ring. After coming out and beginning to speak, Goldberg was quickly interrupted by another episode of Firefly Fun House, where Bray Wyatt told him that he wouldn’t be taking any belt from him at WWE Super ShowDown, and that The Fiend would be talking to him soon.

Quickly after, the lights in the arena went off, and The Fiend appeared behind Goldberg. Unfortunately for him, Goldberg anticipated it, simply turning around and delivering a shocking spear to the scary superstar. Afterwards, the lights went out once again, and The Fiend disappeared, with his laugh echoing in the arena, leaving Goldberg standing there and waiting for their matchup.
