Another Happy Day Trailer: Ellen Barkin vs. Demi Moore

In this corner of Sam Levinson’s debut feature Another Happy Day, you’ve got Ellen Barkin, who’s heading to her oldest son’s wedding after springing his younger brother (Ezra Miller) out of his fourth stint in rehab. In this corner, you’ve got Demi Moore, now married to Barkin’s ex (Thomas Haden Church) and fending off family rumors that she’s a former stripper. Who wins when you toss these two into a wedding (alongside Kate Bosworth and Ellen Burstyn) and let them fight it out to be the alpha female? Well, probably Barkin: Since Happy Day wrapped, she’s been dating the 26-year-old Levinson, trumping even the redoubtable Moore when it comes to real-life May-December romance. [Coming Soon]
