Bachelorette's Reid Rosenthal Surprises on 'Say Yes to the Address'

The most dramatic housing search ever! The Bachelorette season 5 second runner-up Reid Rosenthal helps a couple find their dream home on the Friday, March 25, episode of Say Yes to the Address as seen in Us Weekly’s exclusive sneak peek.

The clip shows Rosenthal, a Philadelphia-based realtor, helping newlyweds Jason and Blair find a new place to begin their life together.

“Reid has been a friend of mine for 10 years or so,” Jason tells the camera. Blair adds, “He’s a very successful real estate agent in Philadelphia.”

Despite Jason’s hopes that they find an abode in the city, Rosenthal says, “I’m going to show you a lot of different options for you to consider, OK? Townhomes, condos, suburban houses.”

The realtor competed for Jillian Harris‘ affection on The Bachelorette season 5, which aired in 2009, but he was sent home in the fantasy-suites episode. Rosenthal memorably surprised Harris by returning during the finale to propose to her, but she nonetheless rebuffed him and gave her final rose to Ed Swiderski. Harris and Swiderski ended their engagement in July 2010, and the Love It or List It Too designer, 36, announced earlier this month that she and boyfriend Justin Pasutto are expecting their first child together.
