Charli Baltimores biography, fact, career, awards, net worth &amp; life story

  • Height: 5′ 5″ (1.65 m)

  • Full Name: Charli Baltimore
  • Nationality: German, American

  • Occupation: Rapper, Singer, Songwriter

Charlie Baltimore’s first appeared in the music industry in the year 1995. She appeared with Junior M.A.F.I.A’s “Get Money Playing”. Baltimore’s first video was released in 1998. When she was a child, she was mostly raised by her elder half-sister, Yolanda. She has two daughters, India and Sianni. Interestingly, Charli Baltimore gave birth to her first daughter when she was 14 years old.

Her first album released in 1999 – she called it “Cold as Ice”. However, there were some label disputes, and so, the album was released to the public till 2009. She left Untertainment, after her album released unofficially. Charlie then did guest recording with artists such as Christina Milian, Mya, Ja Rule, Cam’ron and Fat Boy. While she was doing all this work, she was still releasing singles. Charlie Baltimore has a Grammy nomination for her song “Diary” in 2003. She then started working for The Inc., Records in the year 2011, and her next album True Lies released in late June 2012.
