Who is Vikings Star, Travis Fimmel?

Travis Fimmel is an actor and model based in Australia who came under the media limelight after his TV show Vikings. He played the role of Ragnar Lothbrok in the History Channel series.

Age, Height, Birthday, Parents 

Travis’s birthday fell on July 15, 1979, to parents Chris and Jennie Fimmel. He grew up on a dairy farm in Echuca, Victoria. Fimmel’s father worked as a cattle farmer, and his mother was a recreation officer for people who had a disability. 

Travis wanted to become a Footballer but could not because of a leg injury. Various sources say he enrolled at RMIT University to be an architect but decided to be a traveller instead.

Fimmel is five feet, ten inches tall, with blonde hair and blue eyes. Travis weighs around eighty-six kilograms. He belongs to the Caucasian ethnicity.
