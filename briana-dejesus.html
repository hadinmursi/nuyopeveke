Briana DeJesus News - Us Weekly

Name:Briana DeJesus

Hometown:Orlando, Florida

Relationship:Single

Birthday:May 21, 1994

About

Brian DeJesus first starred on 16 & Pregnant in a 2012 episode. She and her boyfriend at the time, Devoin Austin II, welcomed daughter Nova Star DeJesus on September 10, 2011. In 2013 she appeared on Teen Mom 3, which later was canceled.

In 2017, she welcomed a daughter, Stella Star, on July 2, whom she shares with Luis Hernandez.

She also briefly dated Javi Marroquin and in 2017, MTV announced she would be joining the cast of Teen Mom 2.
