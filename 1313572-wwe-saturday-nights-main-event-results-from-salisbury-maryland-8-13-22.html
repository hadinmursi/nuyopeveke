WWE Saturday Nights Main Event Results From Salisbury, Maryland (8/13/22)

WWE was in Salisbury, MD over the weekend for a live event featuring superstars from both RAW and SmackDown. Check out the results of this Saturday Night’s Main Event below.

  • Bianca Belair defeated Asuka
  • GUNTHER defeated Ricochet
  • Veer Mahaan defeated R-Truth
  • Seth Rollins defeated Dolph Ziggler and continued attacking afterwards. Riddle made the save.
  • Bobby Lashley defeated Theory & The Miz
  • Ronda Rousey & Shayna Baszler defeated Raquel Rodriguez & Aliyah
  • Madcap Moss & The Street Profits defeated Sheamus & The Usos
