That Movie Money

Brad Pitt is best known for being an actor and a talented one at that. Though it took him some time to land huge roles, he would quickly show the world that he was among the best of the bunch. Naturally, he was able to start commanding a hefty salary for the films that he would star in.

Of course, this type of pay does fluctuate at times, as GQ also reports that Pitt cut his salary down to $10 million to appear in the film Once Upon a Time in Hollywood. Still, $10 million is nothing to scoff at for any performer.

Sometimes, however, Pitt can get a bump in pay, and this happened back when he was part of the ensemble cast of Ocean’s Eleven. All in all, Pitt would net himself $30 million for performing in the movie, which is a staggering sum of money!
