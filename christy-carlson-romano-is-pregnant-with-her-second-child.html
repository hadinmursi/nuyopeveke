Christy Carlson Romano Is Pregnant With Her Second Child

Baby on board! Christy Carlson Romano is expecting her second child with her husband, Brendan Rooney, her rep confirms to Us Weekly.

The Even Stevens alum, 34, gushed about her pregnancy on Instagram on Friday, August 31. “We are so excited, we just can’t keep this a secret any longer!” she wrote. “@thebrendanrooney and I are thrilled to announce that we’re expecting again!”

Romano jokingly warned her followers not to tell her and Rooney’s 20-month-old daughter, Isabella Victoria, that she is “gonna have to share her toys soon!”

The former Kim Possible voice actress — who is set to make a cameo in the upcoming movie adaptation of the early aughts Disney Channel series — started dating Rooney in February 2011. The producer proposed seven months later on a gondola in Italy. The couple tied the knot on New Year’s Eve 2013 at a castle nestled in the mountains of Alberta, Canada.
