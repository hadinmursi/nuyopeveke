Miranda Kerr Goes Topless on Harper's Bazaar, Says Loves to Be Naked

Luck be an au naturel lady!

Just in time for Valentine’s Day, Miranda Kerr covers the February 2015 issue of Harper’s Bazaar, inside which she shared quite the romantic—and provocative—truth: She prefers to be naked.

The Aussie model dished that she likes to lounge about in her silk robe—and nothing else. “Just around the house,” she said, adding, “I love taking my shoes off and being in the grass. Or sunbaking naked.”

Unfortunately, she says, the pesky paps get in the way. “I wish I could go skinny-dipping in the ocean. If I didn’t have to worry about anyone taking my picture, I’d totally be out there,” the Swarovski model, 31, admitted. “I quite like to be naked.”

And who could blame her! The former Victoria’s Secret Angel boasts one of the world’s most famous bodies, complete with killer abs, sky blue eyes, and adorable dimples. No wonder it’s been splashed onto billboards for H&M, Bally, Mango, and her own beauty line, Kora Organics.

But while Kerr admitted that she enjoys stripping down, she also said that she doesn’t plan on doing it for anyone but herself anytime soon. At the moment, the only man in her life is her 4-year-old son, Flynn (with ex-husband Orlando Bloom).
