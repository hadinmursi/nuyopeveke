Hannah Ferrier News - Us Weekly

Name:Hannah Ferrier

Hometown:Sydney, Australia

Birthday:November 23, 1986

About

Hannah Ferrier is best known for being a chief stewardess on Bravo’s Below Deck Mediterranean, which premiered in May 2016.

Ferrier was an original cast member on the reality show, which follows the crews of high-end yachts throughout their charter seasons. She remained on the series for five seasons until she was fired by Captain Sandy amid accusations that she had drugs aboard the ship. The Australia native has a prescription for Valium and uses a THC pen to help with anxiety. Even after her firing, Ferrier’s relationships with Captain Sandy and Malia White — the crew member who reported the drugs — have continued to make headlines.

Following her departure from Below Deck, Ferrier cofounded an online superyacht training school, Ocean International Training Academy, with Anastasia Surmava. She welcomed daughter Ava in October 2020 with her fiancé, Josh Roberts.
