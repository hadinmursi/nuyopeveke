Linda Scott net worth: Linda Scott is an American pop singer and actress who has a net worth of $500 thousand. Linda Scott was born in Queens, New York in June 1945. As a singer she released her debut studio album Starlight, Starbright in 1961. Scott also released the albums Great Scott! in 1962, Linda in 1962, and Hey, Look At Me Now! in 1965. Her single “I Don’t Know Why” reached #2 on the US Adult Contemporary chart.
