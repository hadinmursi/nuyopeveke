Michiel Huisman Says &quot;No Thanks&quot; to Playgirl Request

Thanks, but no thanks. Game of Thrones and Age of Adaline hunk Michiel Huisman is flattered by all the attention his shirtless bod has received from the HBO series, but no, he’s not interested in posing for Playgirl. Huisman, who plays Daario Naharis in GoT, told HuffPost Live that he’d never pose for the mag.

“They never reached me. Maybe my team was like, ‘No way’ [before they could ask me],” Blake Lively’s latest costar said when asked about Playgirl’s inquiry. “I’m very flattered by the attention that some of that has gotten but no, I’m not going to do that. Of course not.”
