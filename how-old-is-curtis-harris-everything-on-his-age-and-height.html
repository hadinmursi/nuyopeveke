How Old is Curtis Harris? Everything On His Age And Height

Curtis Harris is 19 years according to his birth date June 27, 2001. And he is 5 ft 8 inches tall.

Curtis Harris is an American actor, voiceover artist, rapper, and writer who is known for his role as Miles Preston in The Haunted Hathaways.

In addition to that, he has also been cast in several roles in a variety of shows and movies which include Justice Baines in L.A.’s Finest, Henry in Doc McStuffins, Scottie in Crash, and Bernstein, Christopher Broyles in Fringe, Young Will in Abraham Lincoln.

As a voice actor, he made his TV show debut by providing vocals to the character of Isaac in an episode titled Freeze Dance Royale/The Boy with the Cardboard Fort of the children’s CGI-animated series, Special Agent Oso, in August 2011.

Curtis Harris’s Nationality

He made his first theatrical film appearance as Young Will in the 2012 action horror film, Abraham Lincoln: Vampire Hunter, starring Benjamin Walker, Dominic Cooper, Anthony Mackie, Mary Elizabeth Winstead, Rufus Sewell, and Marton Csokas.

From 2013 to 2015, Curtis Harris brought to life the character of Miles Preston, the older son of Ray and older brother of Louie who is the most well-mannered of the Preston ghosts, in the television sitcom, The Haunted Hathaways, in which he co-starred alongside Amber Montana, Benjamin “Lil’ P-Nut” Flores, Jr. Breanna Yde, Ginifer King, and Chico Benymon.
