Who is Andy Richters new wife? All about Jennifer Herrera

Andy Richter got married to Jennifer Herrera in 2023. Jennifer is a music executive who has her own company and represents directors for music videos.

The couple got engaged in November 2022 and tied the knot in June 2023 in an intimate ceremony in West Hollywood. Conan O’Brien served as the officiant at the wedding.

Andy Richter is an American actor, comedian, writer, and talk show announcer best known as the sidekick for Conan O’Brien on each of O’Brien’s talk shows: Late Night and The Tonight Show on NBC and Conan on TBS.

Paul Andrew Richter was born in Grand Rapids, Michigan, United States of America. Andy Richter voiced Mort in the Madagascar film franchise.

Andy Richter appeared on the Disney Channel sitcom The Suite Life on Deck as a non-religious hooded brother, Brother Theodore in the episode “Silent Treatment”.

Andy Richter voiced himself in an episode of the 2021 Netflix animated sitcom Chicago Party Aunt. From 2006 to 2008, Richter had a recurring role as “Sad Dad” Stan in the TV show The New Adventures of Old Christine.
