Vince Vaughn Welcomes Baby Boy Vernon Lindsay With Wife Kyla Weber

Vince Vaughn is now the father of a daughter AND a son! The 43-year-old Delivery Man star welcomed his second child, a baby boy named Vernon Lindsay Vaughn, with wife Kyla Weber at a hospital outside of L.A. on Wednesday, Aug. 7, his rep confirms to Us Weekly.

Baby Vernon shares the same name as Vaughn's father. Vaughn and Weber, who wed in January 2010, are already parents to daughter Locklyn Kyla, 2

The actor revealed that he and his wife were expecting their second child during an April 26 interview with Ellen DeGeneres. "This is the first time I've said anything," he said. "We're very excited."

Vaughn recently teamed back up with his 2005 Wedding Crasher's costar Owen Wilson for the June 2013 comedy The Internship — but this time they both came to the set as fathers. "In Wedding Crashers we didn't have little kids visiting the set," Wilson, 44, told Us. "It wouldn't have been appropriate maybe for little kids, but I think on The Internship it was nice. It was nice to have my son visit — and Vince's family."
