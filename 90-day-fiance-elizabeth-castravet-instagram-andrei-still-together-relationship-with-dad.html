90 Day Fiance Star Elizabeth Castravets Instagram Reveals The Truth About Her Complicated Family

Elizabeth and Andrei Castravet's journey to marriage was chronicled on 90 Day Fiance, and things haven't always been smooth for the couple. Fans remember when Andrei fought with Elizabeth's dad. And they have struggled with everything from preparing for their wedding to Andrei's green card status. Now the couple are parents to their two children, Ellie and Winston.

Elizabeth Castravet seems to enjoy keeping her Instagram account updated all the time for her close to 850,000 followers. She posts about her adorable children, her marriage, and sometimes promotes products. What can 90 Day Fiance fans find out from Elizabeth's Instagram about her family life?

What Is 90 Day Fiance Star Elizabeth Castravet's Life With Her Kids Like?

Elizabeth and Andrei Castravet are one 90 Day Fiance couple with kids now. And looking at Elizabeth Castravet's Instagram, she definitely lives a packed life with her two children. She keeps busy maintaining a YouTube account featuring her daughter Ellie called Ellie's World. She also has an Instagram account that is attached to the channel where she promotes her new videos.

Elizabeth filmed Ellie while going through the process of potty training, which can definitely be stressful. And she has many videos of Ellie spending time with her father Andrei Castravet.

Elizabeth has a highlight on Instagram talking about potty training and giving other parents some tips. She said that she and her husband treated Ellie to chocolate every time she went to the bathroom and she said "consistency is key."

Elizabeth also stays busy updating her fans on her life as a mother now that she isn't on 90 Day Fiance anymore. In one Instagram video from May 2023, she shared a tip for putting clutter away with her fanbase: buy a bin from Amazon and then put random things into it that don't really fit anywhere else in the house.

In October 2022, Elizabeth and Andrei had their second child, Winston, and Elizabeth posted about the birth on Instagram.

Elizabeth Castravet has had hard times with her parents and she mentioned it on her Instagram account. In August 2021, Elizabeth posted a photo of her and Andrei sitting in a car and wrote in the caption, "Back when we were headed to my f****** family 'cookout' house."

This was part of her storyline on 90 Day Fiance as her dad and Andrei didn't get along.

Elizabeth explained to Us Weekly in an interview in September 2022 “Let’s just say I’m getting older. I’m more mature. I have my own family and I see things through a different lens now than I used to when I was younger. And so I’m learning more about my family and who’s around me and I just see it differently.”

Elizabeth also said, “It seems like … the more successful you become, you really see people’s true colors that are surrounding you. [My family and I] kind of go back and forth honestly, but it’s been not so great.”

90 Day Fiance's Elizabeth Castravet promotes a few products on Instagram, which suggests that she is making an income from her social media. She has mentioned balancing being a mom and working and how tough that can be. And having two kids most likely makes her life even busier.

What Does Elizabeth Castravet's Instagram Reveal About Her Marriage To Andrei?

Elizabeth and Andrei Castravet may have had rough times in the past. But Elizabeth's Instagram shares how much she cares about him and how well their relationship seems to be going since they appeared on 90 Day Fiance.

In July 2021, Elizabeth shared a video where she wore a black dress and was on her way to go out with Andrei for the evening. She wrote in the caption, "He said: 'baby let me take a video of you.' I love him."

Elizabeth also posts about lots of happy moments with her husband and children, from visits to the pumpkin patch in the fall to beach vacations in Florida.

While all seems great between the couple on Instagram, they have definitely been through some stressful moments. And it seems that the journey to have Andrei stay in the United States hasn't been easy.

When Andrei and Elizabeth Castravet appeared on 90 Day Fiance: Happily Ever After? they had a problem extending Andrei's green card. According to People, Elizabeth was supposed to answer the question of how the couple met, and this caused an issue. Andrei had a green card for two years, but he had to get a new card after that.

Elizabeth explained, "One question that kinda made things kinda take a turn for the worse, it was 'When did you and your husband meet?' and I think that's a very relative question. Like, meet on the phone or face-to-face? So my answer was different and they were like, 'That's not the right answer.' I was like, 'This is my husband. I know when we met. I was there'" according to People.

Even though there are still some challenges in Andrei and Elizabeth's relationship, they seem like they want to stick together and get through it. In 2020, Elizabeth posted on Instagram about their three-year anniversary.

She wrote, "These past 3 years have been the best years of my life. My husband is everything I have dreamed of and more. Eleanor makes our entire family and takes all of our stress and worries away! I love our little family and cannot wait to see what the future holds."
