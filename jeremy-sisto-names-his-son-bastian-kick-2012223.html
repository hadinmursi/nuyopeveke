Jeremy Sisto Names His Son &quot;Bastian Kick&quot;

Add this to the list of unique celebrity baby names!

After welcoming his first son into the world March 9, Suburgatory star Jeremy Sisto's newborn finally has a name.

"Little man finally told us his name; BASTIAN KICK SISTO," the actor tweeted Wednesday.

Last week during his interview on Live! With Kelly, Sisto says that he and his wife Addie Lane, who are parents to daughter Charlie-Ballerina, 2, were having a tough time deciding on a moniker to fit their new addition.

"My wife is stressed about [naming him], but I'm hoping something magical happens that makes me think, 'Oh, well of course this is supposed to be his name,'" he told host Kelly Ripa.

"We got an extension at the hospital, but on Friday [March 16] we do have to go down and put something in, so if we don't have it by then, I think it'll be Baby Boy," he joked. "I think we're gonna be filing extensions for a while. If naming babies is anything like doing homework, then for me it'll take a while to name this kid."

Sisto has been married to Lane since October 2009. The actor's other TV and film credits include Clueless, Six Feet Under, Thirteen and Law & Order.
