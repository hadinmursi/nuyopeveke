Taimi Li is the daughter of great Chinese- Singaporean film actor and martial artist Jet Li.

Taimi’s mother, Huang Qiuyan, is a former Chinese actress. 

She also has a sister, whose name is Si Li. 

The word Taimi comes from Finnish origin, which means “sapling or young tree.”

Taimi’s parents divorced

Taimi’s parents, Jet Li and Qiuyan got married in 1987. 

Unfortunately, things didn’t go well between the former couple, and they got divorced in 1990. 

The couple were together for only three years and had two beautiful children Taimi Li and Si Li.

Siblings

After her parents divorced, her father Jet again got married to Nina Li Chi in 1999, and till now, they are together. 

Taimi has two sisters from her stepmother, whose names are Jada Li and Jane Li. Altogether Taimi has three lovely sisters.

Taimi Loves a simple life

Being the daughter of a celebrity, Taimi loves to have a simple life.

Taimi doesn’t have an interest in show up and high profile life. 

Taimi and her sisters are helping their father to run his charitable One foundation.

Multi-talented father

The famous Jet born in Beijing, China, is a film actor, film producer, martial artist, and retired Wushu champion.

Jet won his first national championship from the Bejingwushu team. 

He made his first debut in the movie Shaolin Temple. 

He has also played martial arts epic films like Zhang Yimou’s Hero (2002), First of Legend (1994), and the series like Once upon a time in China, where he played a folk hero role. 

Jet didn’t limit himself only to the Chinese movies; he showed his talent in an international action film like Kiss of the Dragon, Unleashed, The One, War, Romeo Must die, etc. 

Jet also has Singapore citizenship.

Philanthropic Ambassador

Jet also has done many charitable works to help people. 

He has been the Philanthropic Ambassador of the Red Cross Society of China since January 2006. 

Jet has also worked in mental health by contributing 5,00,000 yuan (US$62,500) of box office revenues from his film Fearless to Red Cross psychological sunshine project.

After having a deathful experience in the Maldives’ 2004 tsunami, Li formed his non-profit foundation called The One Foundation. 

This foundation helps in international disaster relief, mental health awareness, and suicide prevention. 

He was also assigned as the first Goodwill Ambassador. Jet has his wax in Hong Kong Madame Tussauds. 

The action actor has also found a Taiji Zen, a lifestyle company whose mission is to spread “Health and Happiness for ALL” through a balance of physical wellness and mental fitness.
