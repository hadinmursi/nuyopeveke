About Embassy of the United States in Harare, Zimbabwe

American Embassy in Harare runs an inclusive range of consular services to local, American, and international citizens in Zimbabwe.

The American Embassy in Harare supports Zimbabwean citizens through its consular services, if they:

  • Need general or specific info regarding American economy, culture, sports, education and more
  • Need information regarding requirements and process of getting American Citizenship
  • Specific contacts and information in United States
  • Want to apply and obtain American Visa

Visa and Passport services

American passport and visa from are issued only to citizens whose main residence is in United States and Zimbabwe.

The process of issuing American Visa and Passport of United States can take few weeks.

*Please contact with American Embassy in Harare if you have any question as regards of visa and passport matters.

American representations in Zimbabwe

American Embassy in Harare is the only American diplomatic mission in Zimbabwe.

    Zimbabwean representations in United States

    Country of Zimbabwe has 2 other representations in United States:

    • Zimbabwean Permanent Mission in New York
    • Zimbabwean Embassy in Washington DC

    American representations worldwide

    American Embassy In Harare represents one of 257 consular and diplomatic representations of United States all over the world.

    Foreign representations in Zimbabwe

    American Embassy In Harare represents one of 39 foreign consular and diplomatic representations from around the world in Zimbabwe.
