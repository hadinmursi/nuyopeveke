Jamie Dornan's Workout: See the Fifty Shades Star's Insane Biceps!

Fifty Shades? More like 50 reps! It’s no secret that former Calvin Klein underwear model Jamie Dornan is a hunk. After all, the Irish lad was chosen to play Christian Grey in the highly anticipated film adaptation of E L James’ smash erotic novel, Fifty Shades of Grey. But the 32-year-old hottie also works hard to keep his drool-worthy body in shape. 

On Tuesday, Feb. 3, he tweeted a shot doing his Ironfit workout, showing off some crazy-defined, bulging biceps in a challenging pose. 

“With the boys @Ironfit_tv down @T13 . Gave them 2 seconds to take this picture before I collapsed & cried . #ironfit,” he captioned the image.

Ironfit is a company of professional trainers that describe themselves as “strength and movement coaches, nutrition nuts, pro hustlers, iron junkies, hunter G’s, and free range human beings” on their Twitter handle.
