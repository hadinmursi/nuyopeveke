Phil Hendrie Biography and Wiki

Phil Hendrie is an American actor, radio personality and voiceover artist who was born and brought up in Arcadia, California, United States. Currently, he is widely known for his voiceover work on radio and also in popular films and TV shows. Phil rose to fame in the mid-1990s where he hosted his own self-titled show.

Career

Phil rose to fame in the mid-1990s for hosting his own show known as The Phil Hendrie Show. However, his passion for radio began at an early age. He was among four children who were raised in an upper-middle-class family. Thus, he made a demo tape and got his first Dj job at WBJW located in Winter Park. Phil left the station after a while and proceeded to WNOE-FM back in 1978. He worked there until 1988 in Miami, San Diego, Utica, Los Angeles and Fresno.

In 1989, he dropped his debut talk show on KFI that was canceled in 1990. Fortunately, he was offered another job by KVEN in Ventura California. There, he was able to introduce his first fictional character, Iraqi Raj Fahneen on-air. However, The Gulf War had broken out and the real Fahneen defended Sadaam Hussein.

Listeners went ballistic thinking he was the real person on the radio. Nevertheless, he went on to create his own entire cast of characters that evolved in only 18 months. Phil even took the show to prominent stations in Atlanta, Minneapolis and Maimi where his fame grew by the day.
